Welcome to Sofla Milling Limited

At Sofla Milling Limited, we pride ourselves on being a leading provider of high-quality maize products, including maize bran and maize germ. Our dedication to excellence and innovation has established us as a trusted name in the industry.

Founded in [Year], Sofla Milling Limited has grown from a small local mill to a major supplier of maize products. What We Do

We specialize in processing and supplying a variety of maize products. Our state-of-the-art facilities ensure that we produce the finest quality products to meet the needs of our diverse clientele. Our state-of-the-art facilities are equipped with advanced technology and machinery, allowing us to carry out efficient processing and production processes. From cleaning and sorting to milling and packaging, we maintain strict quality control at every step to ensure the purity, freshness, and nutritional integrity of our maize products.
